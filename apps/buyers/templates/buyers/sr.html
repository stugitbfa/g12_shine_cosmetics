Shine Cosmetics – Shipping & Return Policy

  • Shipping Coverage: We offer shipping across India, covering major cities and towns. Unfortunately, we do not offer international shipping at this moment.
  • Return & Exchange Policy:
    • We accept returns or exchanges within 7 days of receipt for damaged or defective items only.
    • The product should be unused, in its original packaging, and with all tags intact.
    • To initiate a return, please contact our customer service at shinecosmetics35@gmail.com with your order details and photos of the product.
    • Return shipping costs are borne by the customer, except for damaged or defective products.
  • Refund Process:
    • Refunds are processed to the original payment method and may take up to 7-10 business days to reflect in your account.
    • In the case of cash-on-delivery orders, refunds will be processed via bank transfer or store credit, based on your preference.
  • Damaged or Incorrect Products: If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please report it within 24 hours of delivery. Contact us at shinecosmetics35@gmail.com with your order number and product details.
  • Important Notes:
    • Returns will not be accepted for products that have been opened or used, except for damaged or defective items.
    • Products bought during sales or promotions are non-returnable unless faulty.
